A message about our cause...

Philosophy Departments are facing closure at several universities after funding cuts and a 17.4% drop in application over the last two years. Like one article noticed: "It seems a course that was good enough for Fyodor Dostoevsky, Albert Camus and even Ricky Gervais is no longer considered to be of value to today's youth."

There is a need in almost every person to search for a truth, and philosophy gives students the chance to do just that- to search for deep question and think in innovative creative and critically way. So, these are all skills that suggest that philosophy students may not be as unattractive to the job market as the government thinks.

We are more then hippies who smoke and drink coffee all day and live in some sort of metaphysical plane and not real world!!!
